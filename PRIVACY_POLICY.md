# Privacy Policy for Eat Journal

**Last Updated: February 14, 2026**

## Introduction

Eat Journal ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices and your privacy rights when using the Eat Journal mobile application (the "App").

## 1. Information We Collect

Eat Journal does **not** save or record any data beyond your local device storage. All information collected through the app is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to external servers or cloud services.

### 1.1 Information Stored Locally on Your Device
- **Photos and Images**: When you use the camera or select photos from your device to document meals
- **Location Data**: Your geographic location when searching for nearby restaurants (used only for search, not recorded)
- **Food Preferences**: Meal entries, cuisine preferences, and restaurant ratings you manually enter
- **Search History**: Restaurants and cuisines you search for or save

### 1.2 Information NOT Collected or Stored
No device information, usage data, or crash reports are collected or stored anywhere. All app functionality operates entirely on your device.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

All information stored in the app is used exclusively on your local device:
- Display your food journal and meal history
- Help you find nearby restaurants based on your location
- Filter and search restaurants by cuisine type

No data is sent to us or any external service for analysis, improvement, or any other purpose.

## 3. Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage Only**: All your photos, journal entries, preferences, and search history are stored exclusively on your device's local storage
- **No External Servers**: Your data is never sent to our servers or any cloud service
- **No Cloud Sync**: Your data is not synced to cloud services by the app itself
- **Device Backups**: If you enable your device's native backup service (iCloud for iOS, Google Drive for Android), your app data may be included in those backups according to your device manufacturer's policies
- **Security**: Since all data remains on your device, you have complete control over your information

## 4. Permissions Explained

### 4.1 Camera Permission
- **Purpose**: To allow you to photograph meals directly within the app
- **Requirement**: Required if you wish to take photos of meals within the app
- **Usage**: Only activated when you choose to take a photo

### 4.2 Photo Library Access
- **Purpose**: To allow you to select existing photos from your device gallery
- **Requirement**: Required if you wish to add photos from your device to your journal
- **Usage**: Only when you choose to add a photo to your journal

### 4.3 Location Permission
- **Purpose**: To identify nearby restaurants when you search
- **Requirement**: Required if you wish to use the restaurant search feature to find nearby locations
- **Usage**: Only when you request to find restaurants near you
- **Note**: Precise location is used only during active searches; we do not track your location continuously

### 4.4 Storage Permission
- **Purpose**: To read and write meal data, photos, and app files to your device
- **Requirement**: Required for basic app functionality

## 5. Third-Party Services

Eat Journal may use the following third-party services:
- **Overpass API**: For restaurant location and search functionality
- **Google Mobile Ads**: For displaying advertisements (if enabled)

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

## 6. Data Sharing

We do **not** share, collect, or have access to any of your personal data. Your photos, meal history, food preferences, and all other data remain exclusively on your device. There is no way for us to access, view, or share your data with third parties because we never receive or store any of it.

## 7. Data Retention

- **While Using the App**: Your data is retained as long as the app is installed
- **After Uninstalling**: Data is permanently deleted from your device
- **Cloud Backups**: If you enable device backups, data may persist in cloud backup services according to their retention policies

## 8. Children's Privacy

Eat Journal is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with information, we will take steps to delete such information.

## 9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your data
- Opt-out of certain data collection

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
